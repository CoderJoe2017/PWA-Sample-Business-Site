Our Mission

We are fee-only advisors who work as consultants, and we provide personal and practice financial advice.

The Kramer Group is a friendly place where dentists and their families can receive excellent, owner-specific cash flow strategies and coaching specifically for small business owners. At The Kramer Group planning takes a very customized approach, there’s nothing “cookie cutter” in our process. We spend time finding out about your personal and business finances so we can create personalized budgeting and cash flow strategies, debt reduction schedules, income tax planning, education planning, insurance assessments, investment allocation analysis, and estate planning.

Our Company

At The Kramer Group our specialty is financial planning for dentists.

We provide in-depth fiduciary financial planning and advice to dentists of all ages, across the U.S. Our services include personal and practice cash flow planning, debt management, financial implications of practice transitions, education funding, insurance assessment, estate planning and the financial "how to's" of retiring.

Let us help you

What is compelling you to address financial planning now? What are your concerns, and What do you hope for?

If you have never worked with a comprehensive fee-only financial planner before, you may be surprised to note there is no sales pitch. We’re not compensated by selling products or gathering assets, so our conversations are focused on strategies, not products, and our focus is on you.
